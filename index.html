🚀 The Evolution of Cars: From Slow to Supercharged

Cars have evolved from slow, boxy machines to hyper-fast beasts. The first-ever automobile, built by Karl Benz in 1885, had only one cylinder—basically a powered bicycle. Now, we have 1,500+ horsepower monsters like the Bugatti Chiron hitting 300+ mph! 🚀🔥

⚡ Major Milestones in Car History ⚡

Year Milestone
1908 🚘 Ford Model T rolls out—first affordable car
1950s 🏎️ Muscle cars dominate the streets
1990s 🚗 Japanese legends like the Nissan Skyline R34 and Toyota Supra take over the tuner scene
2020s ⚡ Electric hypercars like the Rimac Nevera break records
